Building construction is the assembly of structures for residential, commercial, or industrial use. It encompasses design, site preparation, foundation creation, structural framework assembly, utility installation, and finish application. Using materials like concrete and steel, the process requires collaboration among architects, engineers, and laborers to meet safety standards and deliver functional, aesthetically pleasing buildings.
